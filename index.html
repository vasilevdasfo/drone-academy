The honest downsides

So we go in with eyes open.

License. To charge for flights / paid teaching in the US, the instructor needs the FAA Part 107. No license = can't take money for flying.
Insurance & liability. A drone can hurt someone or crash into property. We need liability insurance + a client waiver before the first paid client.
Airspace. We have to confirm the airspace over the terrace is legal to fly (B4UFLY app / LAANC if it's controlled).
The property. Private terrace is our edge over CA's beach/park bans — but we should confirm it's ok to run a business there (zoning / neighbors / noise).
FPV needs a spotter. In goggles you don't keep line of sight, so the law needs a second person watching (Ivan's role).
Fog & weather. The SF coast gets foggy — flexible rescheduling needed.
